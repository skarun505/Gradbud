Common questions from students & parents

Is an MBBS from Moldova valid in India?

Yes. Moldova's medical universities are WHO-listed and recognised by the National Medical Commission (NMC) of India. Graduates need to clear the FMGE/NExT exam to practice in India, which is the same requirement for all foreign medical graduates.

What is the total cost of studying in Moldova?

Total costs (tuition + accommodation + living) typically range from ₹5–8 lakhs per year depending on the program. This is comparable to — and often lower than — private colleges in India, with the added benefit of a European degree and international exposure.

Is it safe for Indian students to live in Moldova?

Moldova is consistently ranked as one of the safer Eastern European countries. There is a well-established Indian student community, and most students find the local population welcoming and friendly.

Do I need NEET to apply for MBBS in Moldova?

While NEET qualification is typically needed to be eligible to study MBBS abroad (as per NMC norms), you do NOT need to crack the NEET cut-off for government seats in India. Our counselors will guide you on the exact requirements based on your specific score and situation.

How quickly can I get an admission offer?

Once your documents are in order, offer letters from Moldovan universities are typically issued within 2–4 weeks. The visa process begins after that and usually takes another 4–6 weeks.
